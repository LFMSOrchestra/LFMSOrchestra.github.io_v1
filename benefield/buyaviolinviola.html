Many parents have asked me for a suggestion on buying a new, upgraded, quality violin. There are many stores in the area that sell violins. Feel free to buy one anywhere you like. I am not telling you where to buy a violin. I am only using my experience to steer you in the right direction. I have found a violin made by Gliga that is imported from Romania. The instrument plays very well throughout the positions and gets a dark rich vibrant sound. In it's price range, it is the best instrument I have heard. Most instruments in the under $1,000.00 price range are heavily varnished and give off a very bright sound. The Gligas give off a dark and rich sound. Again, feel free to buy a violin anywhere you like and any model or brand you like. I have found a store that sells these instruments at an extremely low price. The models are outlined below.
